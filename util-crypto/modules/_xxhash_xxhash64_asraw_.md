**`name`** xxhash64AsRaw

**`summary`** Creates a xxhash non-prefixed hex from the input.

**`description`** 
From either a `string`, `Uint8Array` or a `Buffer` input, create the xxhash and return the result as a non-prefixed hex string.

**`example`** 
<BR>

